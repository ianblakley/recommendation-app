About

This site uses various features of the Spotify API to reflect desired user streaming data such as song/artist recommendations, your top listened to artists/songs, and more.

I created this web app to make a more streamlined user experience with Spotify's web API. As an avid music listener, I'm always looking for more music to listen to and expand my musical palate, and I wanted to bring this experience to other like-minded users.

For more information, feel free to visit my GitHub and send me a message on there. I'm also going to shamelessly plug my LinkedIn here as well if you'd like to learn more about me or connect!
